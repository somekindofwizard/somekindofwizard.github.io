Learn a setlist of random cover songs with people you just met and then perform them - all within one day.

Founded in 2015, Band Swap YEG is a 24+ hour musical charity event based in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada.

Co-founded by Jon Capus (@JonCapus) and Sam Karras (@samandswiss).

How it Works

We put the random bands together on a Friday night and the participants put songs in a hat, drawing at random from a large set of possible bangers, weird deep cuts, or downright guilty pleasures.

The bands then have until Saturday night, when the show must go on, to learn, practice, and rehearse the material. We definitely encourage creativity with songs you may want to spice up, and no one expects you to go on stage knowing all the lyrics and notes perfectly.

Heck, we've been Rick-Rolled before!
